FAQ

What is Woof?

Woof is a Hack Club YSWS where we'll send you a dog plushie or other cool prizes if you ship a project to do with dogs!

What is Hackatime?

We use Hackatime to verify time coded! It is an editor extension that tracks the time you spend coding. You can install it here.

How do I participate?

You need to be 13 to 18 years old to participate! Your identity does need to be verified using Hack Clubs secure accounts portal. A Slack and Github account is also required as well as a demo link (or video for hardware), repo link, screenshot and hackatime project!

What can I build?

Anything to do with dogs! It could be a website, an app, or even a hardware project. Some examples will come out soon™️...
