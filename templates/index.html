FAQ

What is Woof?

Woof is a Hack Club YSWS where we'll send you a dog plushie or other cool prizes if you ship a project to do with dogs!

What is Hackatime?

We use Hackatime to verify time coded! It is an editor extension that tracks the time you spend coding. You can install it here.

How do I participate?

You need to be 13 to 18 years old to participate. More information will be out when the event is confirmed, have a look at Hack Club Slack for updates!

What can I build?

Anything to do with dogs! It could be a website, an app, or even a hardware project. As long as its shipped (accessible via a URL as either a website or executable file, or in the case of hardware is fully built with video demo.) and tracked with Hackatime, we'll take it!
